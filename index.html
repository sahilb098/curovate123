Here’s how Curovate can help you...

REHABILITATION ON YOUR MOBILE DEVICE!

Curovate provides a full rehabilitation program conveniently available through your mobile device in the comfort of your home. Choose your injury or surgery and your graft site for ACL (patellar tendon, hamstring tendon, non-surgical or pre-surgical) and your total hip replacement approach (anterior, posterior, lateral or no hip precautions) or a total knee replacement.

VIDEO GUIDED EXERCISES

The app contains video guided exercises  for every single day of your recovery. What’s even better, the app counts reps and sets for you. No need to write out your exercises or remember what you need to do later. The app will do all of that for you. Curovate provides a customized recovery plan for your specific injury or surgery.

WEEKLY GOALS AND DAILY EXERCISES REMINDERS

Curovate provides specific weekly goals, along with daily reminders to motivate and keep you on track. All of this will help you daily to stay consistent with your exercises and recover fast!

TRACKS YOUR PROGRESS

The app helps you to track your recovery journey and shows daily, weekly and monthly progress to motivate you. If you miss a few days of exercises, don’t worry! Curovate lets you easily resume your recovery and will guide you every step of the way.

FAQ

More FAQ

How can Curovate help my recovery?

Recovery from a major surgery is a long and hard process. Curovate is designed to help you through all of it. You can use it to:

  • Do your daily video guided exercises – as simple as watching a video and following along!
  • Measure your knee or hip range of motion regularly to monitor your progress.
  • Stay motivated by seeing your daily and weekly achievements.
  • Reach out to us if you have any questions on social or at info@curovate.com.

Why should I subscribe to Curovate?

To help you every day with your recovery! There is nothing like Curovate that provides you with video guided exercises, progress tracking, measuring capabilities, and reminders – all on your phone. Curovate provides exactly what you need for your surgery and stage of recovery! Curovate is designed by a physical therapist with input from orthopaedic surgeons and other physical therapists. Curovate is also the result of 4 years of hard work and input from hundreds of patients. Our app does not contain advertising and we do not sell your data.

I got really busy and missed a few days of exercise. What do I do?

If you miss a few days, Curovate will wait for you! You can resume your exercises and recovery right where you left off. No passive aggressive pop-ups to make you feel bad, we promise!

Are the exercises the same every week?

NO! Curovate provides you with daily exercises for 6 months of rehabilitation. Typically, the exercises and reminders change every week. As you progress, you will be provided with more challenging exercises. That’s 6 months of daily exercises for ACL, total knee and total hip replacement right in your hand!
